Conference- 12th & 13th Nov

NCPCC 2016 Mumbai, The Pediatric Critical Care Conference with a Difference, the theme being “Less Invasive……. More Intensive “. It is an all-inclusive conference, addressing the issues that are approaching the frontiers of science and ideas that have moved from bench to bedside. It aims to fine tune the day to day practices including fluid therapy, inotropes, antimicrobial use, ventilation strategies, RRT in the background of recent guidelines and landmark publications that you may not have had the time to read.

Unique features of this conference include, a special full day track dedicated to reaching out to the difficult-to-reach critically ill child in villages, talukas and districts where 80% of our children live. If you are a pediatrician coping with this difficult task, you must attend to discuss and evolve collective solutions with the most ingenious minds in pediatric critical care.

There are 10 workshops to choose from, each providing an opportunity for hands on learning under the guidance of international faculty and national experts. The Pre-conference CMEs address critical care issues in case-based interactive format (“Chaat sessions”) without didactic lectures.

Most important of all, we plan to provide several platforms for one-to-one interactions between delegates and experienced faculties for you to ask questions that you may not have asked or got a chance to ask, during the sessions. Welcome to the warmth and vibrancy of Mumbai at a conference without boundaries & barriers.
